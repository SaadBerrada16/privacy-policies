# Privacy Policy — Quran Finder

**Last updated: April 17, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how Quran Finder ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Microphone (Audio Recording)
The App requests access to your device's microphone solely to enable the **verse recognition feature**, which allows you to identify a Quran verse you are currently listening to. Audio is processed entirely **on-device** using a local speech recognition model. We do not record, store, transmit, or share your voice data with any external server or third party.

### Internet Access
The App connects to the internet only to:
- Stream Quran audio recitations from public, openly licensed CDN endpoints.
- Fetch verse text and tajweed annotation data from the Quran CDN public API (`api.qurancdn.com`).

No personal information is included in these requests. No user account or login is required.

---

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:
- Names, email addresses, or any other personally identifiable information.
- Location data.
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs.
- Usage analytics or crash reports sent to external services.
- Any audio recordings or transcripts from the microphone.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following external services:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Quran CDN (`api.qurancdn.com`) | Verse text and audio URLs | Public API, no user data transmitted |

No third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries are included in the App.

---

## 4. Permissions

| Permission | Why it is needed |
|---|---|
| `RECORD_AUDIO` / Microphone | On-device verse recognition; audio never leaves the device |
| `INTERNET` | Stream audio and fetch verse data |

---

## 5. Data Retention

Because the App does not transmit or store personal data, there is no data to retain or delete. All temporary audio captured for recognition is discarded immediately after processing and never written to disk.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App contains no user accounts, no social features, and no data collection mechanisms.

---
